About Me

Rooted in kindness, creativity, and connection.

I’m originally from the beautiful mountains of Nepal and now call Melbourne my second home. I bring a calm, caring presence into every classroom and love building strong, trusting relationships with children, families, and colleagues.

My teaching is shaped by respect, togetherness, and joyful learning. Whether we are exploring a butterfly life cycle, solving a numeracy game, or simply talking through a big feeling, I want children to feel confident, capable, and loved.

Teaching Style

Playful learning with purpose.

  • Play-based and intentional teaching
  • Warm guidance during routines and transitions
  • Documentation through observations and learning stories
  • Inclusive practice aligned with EYLF, VEYLDF, and NQS
  • Open, caring communication with families
